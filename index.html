Boxing Philosophy

A sporting and leisure program of a special kind. In small groups, we would like to give you the opportunity to get to know boxing and its philosophy

No other sport motivates and promotes co-ordination skills, rhythm, responsiveness, speed and endurance like boxing. Learn the correct technique and footwork in partnership and with individual support, and get motivated by the dynamism of the group when working on the punchbag.

You will experience it ... the sport of boxing increases your performance and assertiveness and develops an improved mental attitude which can be transferred to many other areas of life.

Enjoy a leisure training that besides increasing the general fitness and training of the boxers Skills together is mainly a lot of fun.

We offer basic training, which is to increase the general fitness and improve the boxer skills, above all also a lot of fun. In individual workouts and also with a partner you will learn effective impact connections and combinations with the corresponding footwork, condition set-up, increase of strength-endurance, speed, coordination and reflexes.

If you want to try out boxing, you can take part in an introductory course. We offer you an introduction to the basic techniques and the corresponding footwork for shadowboxing, boxing equipment and partner work without any risk of injury.

Individual training is geared to their own challenges, goals and performance. The personal training is particularly effective as you will be individually cared for your strength, endurance, coordination and reflexes very quickly. So you can. e.g. With your partner or friends, and also train regularly on the basis of individually tailored training plans.

Are you planning a special event or want to try something new with friends? Try your hand at boxing ...
We leave you our boxing gym for an entire training unit and you get your own boxing trainer, who will give you an introduction to the art of boxing.
Learn how to work in partnership and without the risk of injury Get into the techniques of striking techniques and the corresponding footwork and let yourself be carried away by the dynamism of the group.

More and more women are discovering the sport of boxing to increase fitness, coordination and speed. We offer a separate course for women, where you can try first without any performance pressure. The content is, of course, the same, but always the achievement of your own goals is the challenge.
